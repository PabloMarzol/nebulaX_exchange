# Crypto assets

Onramp now supports more than 450 tokens/coins and 30 networks, and it's continuously expanding its offerings. Here are a few networks that are currently supported for onramp transactions:

{% hint style="info" %}
**Note**:

* **All coins and Network:** To obtain a list of available coins, please refer to the `listAllCoins` [link](https://docs.onramp.money/onramp/onramp-widget-integration/allconfig-all-coin-details). For information on their supported networks, refer to the `coinNetworks` [link](https://docs.onramp.money/onramp/onramp-widget-integration/allconfig-all-coin-details/coinnetwork-get-all-supported-networks).
* **Supported Assets:** Please refer to the dedicated page on our official website, [Supported Tokens by onramp.money](https://onramp.money/supported-token/). This page offers a continuously updated and current listing of all tokens and coins available for purchase or exchange on our platform.
* For USD only the following assets are available:\
  \- USDC on ETH, POL, SPL, ARB, BASE, XLM, OP, AVAXC\
  \- USDT on ETH\
  \- PYUSD on ETH
  {% endhint %}
